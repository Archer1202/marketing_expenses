# marketing_expenses
Analysis of ticket sales for events during the period 2017-2018 to find areas of opportunity and redistribution of resources,. A significant resource drain and an area of ​​opportunity to be explored were found.

#The project uses the Python language with Juppy Notebook for ease of use and implementation.

#The Matplotlib, Pandas, Numpy, Seaborn, and Datetime libraries are used for this project

#The project involves generating cohorts to search for dates and sales channels in the first instance to see which are the key sales dates; the DAU, WAU and MAU metrics are extracted to see the patterns

#The second part looks at the number of returning customers, those who are loyal, and identifies opportunities to grow that number, as well as the number of days it takes for them to make purchases and average purchase volumes.

#The final section of the project shows the marketing expenditure generated by various channels, along with its breakdown. This information is used to generate analyses of CAC, ROMI, and LTV, among others, to compare sales channels and their advertising and identify areas of opportunity. Everything is illustrated and shown with graphs.
